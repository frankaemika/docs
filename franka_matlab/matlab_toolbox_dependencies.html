Matlab toolbox dependencies

The following Mathworks products are required:

  • Matlab (Compatibility with Matlab & libfranka)

  • Simulink

  • Simulink Coder

Some of demos provided with the franka_matlab could potentially need either of the following toolboxes:

  • Matlab Coder (cartesian_impedance_control.slx, force_control.slx)

  • Stateflow (grasp_object.slx)

  • Matlab Robotics Toolbox with Matlab R2021b or greater (pick_and_place_with_RRT.mlx)
